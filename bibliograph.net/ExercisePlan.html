ExercisePlan

schema:Thing > schema:MedicalEntity > schema:MedicalTherapy > schema:LifestyleModification > schema:PhysicalActivity > schema:ExercisePlan
schema:Thing > schema:CreativeWork > schema:ExercisePlan

Fitness-related activity designed for a specific health-related purpose, including defined exercise routines as well as activity prescribed by a clinician.
