VideoGame

schema:Thing > schema:CreativeWork > schema:SoftwareApplication > schema:VideoGame
schema:Thing > schema:CreativeWork > schema:Game > schema:VideoGame

A video game is an electronic game that involves human interaction with a user interface to generate visual feedback on a video device.
