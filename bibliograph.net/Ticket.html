Ticket

schema:Thing > schema:Intangible > schema:Ticket

Used to describe a ticket to an event, a flight, a bus ride, etc.
PropertyExpected TypeDescription
Properties from schema:Ticket
schema:dateIssued schema:DateTime The date the ticket was issued.
schema:issuedBy schema:Organization The organization issuing the ticket or permit.
schema:priceCurrency schema:Text The currency (in 3-letter ISO 4217 format) of the price or a price component, when attached to PriceSpecification and its subtypes.
schema:ticketNumber schema:Text The unique identifier for the ticket.
schema:ticketToken schema:URL  or
schema:Text 		Reference to an asset (e.g., Barcode, QR code image or PDF) usable for entrance.
schema:ticketedSeat schema:Seat The seat associated with the ticket.
schema:totalPrice schema:Number  or
schema:Text  or
schema:PriceSpecification 		The total price for the reservation or ticket, including applicable taxes, shipping, etc.
schema:underName schema:Person  or
schema:Organization 		The person or organization the reservation or ticket is for.
